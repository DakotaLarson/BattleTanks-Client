How to Play

BattleTanks is a 2.5D tank shooter where two teams compete in a team eliminiation gamemode!

Default Controls

W - Forward
Mouse - Shoot
S - Backward
R - Reload
A - Rotate Left
E - Ramming Speed
D - Rotate Right
Enter - Chat

Player Details

Hits to Kill: 5
Lives: 3
Points per Hit: 1
Points per Kill: 2

Creating an Arena

You can create an arena and submit it on our Discord Server!

Guidelines

Arenas should have at least four (4) team spawns per team. Currently, all arenas can be used by up to eight (8) people.
Additionally, please avoid one (1) block "hallways". These are currently difficult to traverse when driving.

Creation Tips

I recommend odd dimensions so there is a center block in the arena.
The tools you use can be selected by a hotkey instead of having to use your mouse.
Remember Your primary mouse button creates an entity; your secondary mouse button removes an entity if there is one of that type at the position you are selecting.

Submission & Reward

Upon submission to the Discord Server, I will reivew your arena and offer feedback if necessary. If all looks good, then I will include your arena with the next release of BattleTanks!
Upon approval of your arena, you will granted access to the "Arena Creators" role on the Discord server! I will also note you as the author of the arena so appropriate credit can be made visible in future releases.
